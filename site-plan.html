Overview

Purpose

Our goal is to get everyone experiencing the thrill of whitewater! Regardless of experience or mobility we want you out on the water. Our trustworthy guides will get even the grumpiest of grouches to enjoy themselves and have a good time as their group navigates a rushing, wild river! Prepare to get splashed!

Audience

Everyone! But we are particularly fond of families and groups that are looking to form stronger comraderie even if mobility issues would be a problem elsewhere for members of the group.

Typography

Heading Font: Fjalla One

It has a bold but clean look, making it easy to read and gives distinct, leading headings.

Paragraph Font: Krub

Again, as a sans serif font, it provides a clean, easy to read font.
It's also a lighter script making it easy to follow as main text from the headers.
